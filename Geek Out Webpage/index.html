Geek-Out is a transformation of the card-based party game, Geek Out!, an application that can be played virtually. Players are able to join virtual game rooms via a room code and play width friends no matter where they are!

Geek-Out implements a simple user interface that allows players to play their favorite card game without feeling confused or indifferent as the application is extremely easy to use.


Here are the rules for the game:

Players take turns selecting questions. A die is rolled to select a category. If the die comes up blank, then the player can choose the category. A question is then selected from the given category. Players then bid on who can come up with the most answers for a given question. (Questions are phrased in a way to allow this bidding, like How many Ironman villains can you name?) Once all but one player has passed on bidding, the player who won the bid gets 60 seconds to provide as many answers as they can think of. The other players are then given an opportunity to contest the answers provided. If all of the valid answers left are more than the bid, then the players wins their bid in points. The next player then takes their turn selecting a question. This cycle continues until any player earns more than 10 points. That player is the winner of the game.
